Every detail — the linen draping each terrace, the handmade ceramics at breakfast, the scent of wild thyme drifting from the hillside — is considered with the singular intent of making you feel utterly at ease.

Where to Find Us

Gerakini,
Halkidiki

Nestled between pine-covered hills and the crystalline waters of the Thermaic Gulf, Gerakini is one of Halkidiki's most intimate villages — far enough from the crowds, close enough to everything.

  • 2 km from Gerakini village centre
  • 45 minutes from Thessaloniki Airport (SKG)
  • Private jetty with mooring for charter vessels
  • 10 minutes to Vourvourou sailing hub
  • 1 hour from Mount Athos peninsula
  • Nearest helipad: Nea Moudania (20 min drive)
